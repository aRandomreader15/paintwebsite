Paint is an complex thing that is vital to making your house pop. If you don't know anything about paint, fret not. I got you. The first step is figuring out what you want to paint. Which rooms, and what walls? Once you've figured that out, measure the space on your walls to get your square footage. The amount of paint you get will depend on how much square feet your rooms are sized. Then, figure out what finish you want to purchase. That's the shininess of your paint, how much it reflects. Next, choose which colour you want in your paint. Lastly, figure out the quality of paint you'd like to purchase, if you're unsure of what to get, ask an associate at whatever store you visit.
